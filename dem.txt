DEM :A digital elevation model is a 3D computer graphics representation of elevation data to represent terrain, commonly of a planet, moon, or asteroid.
	 A "global DEM" refers to a discrete global grid. DEMs are used often in geographic information systems, and are the most common basis for digitally produced relief maps.
